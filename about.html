About Me

I am an Industrial & Experience Designer specializing in consumer electronics, currently working on forward-looking product concepts at Huawei, with a focus on redefining products from their core essence and real user behaviors. I believe meaningful innovation doesn’t come from adding more features, but from understanding the most natural ways people interact with objects—and designing experiences that are rich but simple.

Outside of work, I love traveling, hiking, and documenting life through photography—capturing nature, animals, and the human stories hidden in different cities.
